Introduction to Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling.

Harry Potter Movies

The Harry Potter film series is based on the seven novels and consists of eight fantasy films. The films star Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, Rupert Grint as Ron Weasley, and Emma Watson as Hermione Granger.

Harry Potter Books

The Harry Potter books chronicle the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

  • Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
  • Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  • Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  • Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  • Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix
  • Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
  • Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Main Characters

Harry Potter

Harry James Potter is the titular protagonist of the series. He is known as "The Boy Who Lived" and is famous for his lightning-shaped scar.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Jean Granger is one of Harry's best friends. She is exceptionally intelligent and always scores top marks in her classes.

Ron Weasley

Ronald Bilius Weasley is another of Harry's best friends. He comes from a large family and has a great sense of humor.
